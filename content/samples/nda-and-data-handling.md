# Confidentiality and data handling

## Scope

This policy applies to all employees and contractors who process information belonging to the organisation or its customers.

## Personal data

Personal data may only be stored in approved systems listed in the internal register. Do not keep copies of personal data on personal devices or unapproved cloud drives.

## Retention

Project records that contain personal data must be deleted **within 90 days** after project closure unless a longer retention is required by law. Legal holds override automatic deletion.

## Breach reporting

Suspected data breaches must be reported to **security@company.example** within **24 hours** of discovery. Do not discuss suspected breaches publicly until the incident response team confirms external communication.

## Non-disclosure

All confidential information shared during employment remains protected under the signed confidentiality undertaking for **five years** after the end of the engagement.
