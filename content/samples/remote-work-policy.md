# Remote and hybrid work policy

## Eligibility

Team members may work remotely up to three days per week when their role does not require on-site presence. Your manager must approve the recurring pattern in writing.

## Equipment and security

Company laptops must run full-disk encryption and the latest security patch level defined by IT. Public Wi‑Fi is prohibited for accessing internal systems unless the corporate VPN is active.

## Availability

Core collaboration hours are **10:00–15:00 CET** on business days. Outside these hours, responses to chat messages are expected by the next business morning unless a different SLA is agreed for your team.

## Expenses

Home internet costs are not reimbursed unless your contract explicitly states otherwise. Co-working space requires prior approval from finance.
