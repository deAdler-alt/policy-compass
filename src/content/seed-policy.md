# Internal team policy (sample)

## Submission deadline

All project materials must be submitted by **23:59 UTC on the final hackathon day**. Late submissions are not accepted unless an exception is granted in writing by the organisers.

## Confidentiality

Team members must not share unpublished code, datasets, or API keys outside the approved repository. A mutual non-disclosure understanding applies to all internal documents marked as confidential.

## Access and accounts

Production credentials must never be committed to Git. Use environment variables and a `.env` file that is excluded from version control. Rotate any key that was accidentally exposed.

## Support

For compliance questions, contact the programme office using the official channel listed in the participant handbook.
